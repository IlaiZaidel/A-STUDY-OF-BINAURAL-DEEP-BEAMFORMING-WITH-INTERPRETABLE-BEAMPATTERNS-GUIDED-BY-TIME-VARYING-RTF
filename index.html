Audio Samples – Binaural Deep Dynamic Beamforming

Audio samples accompanying the paper:
"A Study of Binaural Deep Beamforming with Interpretable Beampatterns Guided by Time-Varying RTF"

The proposed framework employs continuously tracked Relative Transfer Functions (RTFs) to guide the estimation of the time-varying beamformer weights in dynamic acoustic environments. The RTFs provide spatial cues that enable the network to adapt its beampatterns in real time and maintain focus on the moving speaker. The system generates a binaural output that preserves the spatial characteristics of the target source, promoting natural listening and supporting applications such as hearing aids and hearable devices.
